How I Work

My process, from brief
to brilliant content.

Every video I create starts with understanding — and ends with something your audience remembers.

01

Understand the Project

I immerse myself in your product — reading docs, exploring the protocol, asking questions. I don't create content I don't understand. This is where most creators cut corners; this is where I go deeper.

02

Define the Audience & Message

Who are we talking to? A crypto native? A curious newcomer? The message, tone, and framing shift entirely based on who's watching. I map this before I write a single word of script.

03

Script with Story & Structure

Every video is a story. I script with a hook, a clear arc, and a punchy close. No jargon dumping. No rambling. Just clean, confident communication that respects your viewer's time.

04

Record, Edit & Deliver

From recording with clear audio and intentional visuals, to editing with pacing and motion in mind — every frame is considered. I deliver content that's platform-ready and on-brand, every time.

05

Review, Refine & Repeat

I welcome feedback and iterate with speed. After delivery, I track performance and use real data to inform the next piece. My work gets better with every collaboration.
